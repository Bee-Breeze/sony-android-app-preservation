# Publication privacy review

Decision: passed.

The two exact PNG files were reviewed at original pixels and with OCR. They show
only generic Clock UI in a disposable Android user. No account, contact, message,
calendar item, notification text, location, network name, stable device ID,
secret, personal label, or private media is visible. PNG metadata and trailing
bytes were checked. Residual risk is very low and limited to generic date, time,
status icons, app version context, and Traditional Chinese UI.
